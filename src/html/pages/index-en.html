About Us

From a Small Store to a Large Chain "Raduga" — for the Russian-speaking Community in Korea

Raduga started its journey in 2019 with a small store in the city of Pyeongtaek.

Today, we are a large chain of stores for the Russian-speaking community in Korea, providing thousands of families with quality products.

Supporting the Russian-speaking Community

We strive to be close to the people: we regularly organize events, take part in community life, and support cultural and sports initiatives. We also sponsor sports events to contribute to the development of an active and friendly Russian-speaking community.

Product Quality and Accessibility

Quality, affordability, and price are the foundation of our policy. We carefully select suppliers and control product quality so that our customers receive the best service and can choose from the widest assortment at optimal prices.

Innovation and Growth

We continuously implement new technologies, improve services, and optimize processes to offer you a more convenient, fast, and modern shopping experience. From online delivery to new store formats — we move forward together with you.
